## Selected Approach
**Approach 1 — Technical/Engineering Aesthetic** is chosen. It mirrors the character of the project itself: a handmade PCB, embedded systems, lab work. The yellow-on-dark palette pulls directly from the board photo, making the page feel cohesive and intentional without being decorative.
